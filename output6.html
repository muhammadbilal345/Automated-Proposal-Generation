Problem Statement:

We are looking for a developer or a company to help us build an AI chat bot integrated with Chat GPT to manage a conversation with a prospect and book appointments for local home services companies, such as plumbing companies, HVAC, barber shops, and beauty salons. Convo Flow will be provided but in general it’s the following; 1. Bot gets a message from a potential customer 2. Confirms details of the special / service 3. Confirms Contact necessary contact info for the appointment 4. Confirms available appointment date and time (based on a calendar) 5. Follow up agent with consumers that stopped responding. Requirements: - Develop an AI chat bot that can manage a conversation, answer questions related to (i) company, (ii) services, and (iii) specials - Train the chat bot with provided materials, including (i) info about the company, (ii) company FAQ, (iii) services FAQ, (iv) special FAQ, and (v) generic services FAQ - Ensure the chat bot does not use generic information from the internet to answer related questions to the service or special -A conversational flow will be provided, where the bot gets a message from a potential customer, confirms details of the special/service, confirms necessary contact info for the appointment, and confirms available appointment date and time based on a calendar - Develop a POC for the first 3 stages without any integration - Build the final project with a "micro services" approach and the ability to teach the bot by providing basic information for every company & niche - Integrate the service with a CRM called Go High Level - Ensure the bot can integrate with any CRM/solution using API calls - Store all conversations and run analytics to understand the cost of each conversation - Provide monthly billing for X conversations/leads and sell packages for additional conversations - Deliver the project on the agreed-upon date and a working solution - Issue a full refund in case the solution does not work as expected and provides false information Mention guru to be considered Deliverables: - POC should be deployed on a solution of your choice where we can test it’s abilities to manage a conversation. - Full documentation - Possible to work with us after the project is completed and improve the product
